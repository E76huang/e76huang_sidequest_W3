## Project Title

Side Quest 3: "Lost Puppy Adventure" Descion based Story Game

---

## Description

> In Side Quest 3, players are given two buttons to choose from: one represents a good choice, the other a bad choice.
> Depending on which option they select, the game will lead to either a good or bad ending.

---

## Interaction Instructions

> Enter / I on the start screen. Press R on win/lose to return.
> Click "Start" button the begin the story
> Click on one of the two button choices to progress to the next set of descions
> Reach at least 3 "Good Descion" points to acheive Good ending, otherwise players reach the Bad ending
